Level 2 observations: NovelD behavior snapshots

I used lander x position versus lander y position as the behavior snapshot.
This shows which parts of the LunarLander-v3 state space the policy visits at
early, middle, and late points in training.

The later NovelD snapshot shows trajectories on both sides of the landing zone
instead of staying on one narrow path. That is what I expected from a reward
that only pays for moving into a state that is more novel than the previous one.

The main thing I learned is that behavior snapshots are more informative than a
reward curve for exploration. If the visited-state cloud spreads out but return
does not improve, then the exploration bonus is working mechanically, but it is
not helping the task. On LunarLander this diagnostic is more useful, because
the agent can fail by repeatedly exploring unsafe arcs instead of learning to
settle near the pad.
